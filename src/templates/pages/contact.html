Ciscon provides a wide range of services to the oil and gas industry, including:

  • Geological Services
  • Engineering Services
  • Training Services
  • Consulting Services
  • Safety Equipment Supply
  • Technical Support

Each of these service areas is supported by a team of experienced professionals with extensive industry knowledge.

Ciscon operates from four centers:

  • Headquarters in Port Harcourt, Nigeria
  • Branch office in Warri, Nigeria
  • Branch office in Lagos, Nigeria (Liaison center)
  • International office in Houston, Texas, USA

Each location is strategically positioned to serve our clients throughout Nigeria and internationally.

Ciscon has been in operation since 1986, providing over 30 years of service to the oil and gas industry in Nigeria and beyond. Over this time, we have built a reputation for excellence, quality, and reliability in all our operations.

Ciscon has attained ISO 9001:2001 quality management certification, demonstrating our commitment to maintaining the highest quality standards in all our operations. We also hold certifications from the Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR) and the Standards Organization of Nigeria (SON), among others.
