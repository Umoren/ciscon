Well Site Geology

Our well site geology services provide real-time geological monitoring and analysis during drilling operations. Our experienced geologists work on-site to collect, analyze, and interpret geological data to optimize drilling decisions and maximize reservoir understanding.

  • Sample Collection and Analysis

    Systematic collection and analysis of drill cuttings and core samples to identify lithology, formation boundaries, and potential reservoir zones.

  • Real-time Formation Evaluation

    Continuous monitoring and interpretation of drilling parameters to identify formation changes and potential hydrocarbon indicators.

  • Wellsite Decision Support

    Expert geological input for critical drilling decisions, including coring points, casing points, and well trajectory adjustments.

Mud Logging

Our mud logging services provide detailed analysis of drilling mud and cuttings to identify potential hydrocarbon reservoirs. Using advanced equipment and techniques, our mud loggers monitor and record key parameters throughout the drilling process to enhance formation evaluation and well control.

  • Gas Detection and Analysis

    Continuous monitoring of drilling mud for hydrocarbon gases to identify potential reservoirs and assess their composition.

  • Drilling Parameter Monitoring

    Recording and analysis of drilling parameters to optimize drilling performance and identify potential drilling hazards.

  • Formation Evaluation

    Detailed analysis of cuttings and mud properties to determine lithology, porosity, permeability, and fluid content of formations.

Geological Consulting

  • Field Development Planning

Reservoir Characterization

Our reservoir characterization services provide detailed analysis of reservoir properties to support field development and production optimization. Combining geological, petrophysical, and engineering data, we develop comprehensive reservoir models that guide development decisions.

  • Core Analysis

    Detailed examination of core samples to determine reservoir properties, including porosity, permeability, and fluid saturation.

  • Petrophysical Analysis

    Integration of well log data, core data, and production data to develop detailed reservoir property models.

  • Reservoir Modeling

    Development of static and dynamic reservoir models to support field development planning and production optimization.

Geological Mapping

Our geological mapping services provide detailed maps and models of subsurface structures and stratigraphy to support exploration and field development. Using advanced software and interpretation techniques, we create accurate geological maps that enhance understanding of petroleum systems.

  • Structural Mapping

    Detailed mapping of structural features such as faults, folds, and traps to identify potential hydrocarbon accumulations.

  • Stratigraphic Mapping

    Correlation and mapping of stratigraphic units to understand reservoir distribution and connectivity.

  • 3D Geological Modeling

    Creation of three-dimensional geological models that integrate surface and subsurface data for comprehensive understanding of petroleum systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our geological services

Our geological services primarily cover Nigeria and the Gulf of Guinea region, including both onshore and offshore areas. We have extensive experience in the Niger Delta Basin, Chad Basin, and other sedimentary basins in West Africa. Our team's local knowledge of these regions, combined with international expertise, allows us to provide high-quality geological services across diverse geological settings.

We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and software for geological analysis, including advanced mud logging units, gas detection systems, and microscopic analysis tools. For data interpretation and modeling, we use industry-standard software such as Petrel, Techlog, Kingdom, and Landmark, among others. Our team is continuously trained on the latest technologies to ensure we deliver the most accurate and comprehensive geological analyses.

Our geological services are designed to integrate seamlessly with other disciplines to provide comprehensive subsurface solutions. We work closely with geophysicists to ensure consistency between geological interpretations and seismic data. Similarly, we collaborate with reservoir engineers to develop geological models that support reservoir simulation and performance prediction. This integrated approach ensures that all aspects of subsurface characterization are aligned and contribute to optimal decision-making.

The timeline for geological studies varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Basic studies such as well site geology and mud logging are conducted in real-time during drilling operations. More comprehensive studies, such as detailed reservoir characterization or basin analysis, typically take 2-6 months to complete. We work closely with our clients to establish realistic timelines and deliver results according to project schedules.

Quality and accuracy are paramount in our geological services. We ensure this through several measures:

  • Adherence to ISO 9001:2001 quality management standards
  • Employment of experienced geologists with advanced degrees and industry certifications
  • Regular calibration and maintenance of all equipment
  • Rigorous QA/QC procedures for data collection and analysis
  • Peer review of all interpretations and reports before delivery to clients
  • Continuous professional development and training for our geological team
These measures ensure that our geological services consistently meet the highest standards of quality and accuracy.
