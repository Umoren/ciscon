Our Capabilities

Comprehensive Liner Hanger Solutions

We offer various types of liner hanger products to meet your specific well requirements

RPSB Liner Hanger

Our model RPSB hanger offers superior performance through innovative design:

  • Unique tongue and groove slip design for superior performance
  • Reduced potential cone collapse due to unique slip and hanger body design
  • Protected slip while running in
  • Hydraulic set (mechanical set also offered)
  • Hanging capacities beyond standard slip and cone liner hangers

Setting Tool Features

Our setting tools are designed for reliable operation:

  • Run on model D setting tool (mechanical release)
  • Solid body and welded body construction
  • Designed to maximize circulating area
  • 8rd and buttress threads are standards
  • L80 and P110 are standards

Liner Top Packers

Our liner top packers provide exceptional sealing capabilities:

  • Weight set packers (except the HLT-H)
  • Can be set at any time during cementing operation using a tamping dog assembly
  • Element is secured in the set position by an internal lock ring
  • Built with slips for higher hanging capabilities

Liner Tieback Packer

Our tieback packers ensure reliable connections:

  • Run in a second trip after cementing and liner setting tool retrieval
  • Bottom seal nipple stings into an existing tie back on top of a previously set liner
  • Hydraulic or mechanical set design
  • High temperature and high pressure design
  • Anti-swab packer element design
  • Full bore packer
Additional Solutions

Specialized Liner Hanger Components

Our range of liner hanger components is designed to provide complete and reliable liner installation solutions.

Tieback Seal Assembly

  • Consists of seal mandrel, seal units, half mule shoe, spacers as required by tie back length, and a locator
  • Mule Shoe configuration aids in stabbing and provides smooth re-entry for wire line equipment
  • Seal units to meet any well environment
  • Manufactured from alloy steel to meet liners properties
  • Optional cement port available

Hydraulic Running Tool

  • Provides right hand rotation of string in tension, compression or neutral position
  • After actuating the hydraulic piston, the tool can be retrieved by straight pull
  • Secondary release mechanism: approximately 6 turns with left hand rotation
  • Precludes accidental release

Cement Manifold

  • Suspends the drill pipe weight from the top drive assembly
  • Heavy duty swivel permits manipulation of the work string without breaking down the cement line
  • Connects the cementing line to the running string during liner cementing operation
  • Retains the drill pipe plug for release after the cement job
  • External valve directs fluid flow either above or below the plug
  • Ball drop sub available for use with a hydraulic setting tool
