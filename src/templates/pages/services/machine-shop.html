Precision fabrication and machining services for the oil and gas industry

Mazak Slant Turn Series

Slant Turn Slant Turn

Mazak's Slant Turn CNC lathe, with an up to 15-station tool turret, is engineered for heavy duty cutting of large workpieces up to 33" in diameter. It has a massive, rigid wide slant bed with a machine weight ranging from 10 to 13 tons. With a three geared spindle motor, heavy cutting is available at any speed. Large spindle bore capacity makes this machine a real workhorse for the oil services industry.

SLANT TURN MAIN CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Large, slant-bed design
  • Wide range of chuck options
  • Large bore capacity, through hole chucks
  • Long bed lengths available
  • Mazatrol conversational CNC

Mazak Powermaster Series

Power Master Series

These are some of the biggest turning centers on the market today. Designed for heavy duty cutting of large workpieces, the Powermaster have a flat-bed construction for easy access to your part. Plus, both have big, hollow bore capacity for large diameter pipes.

POWERMASTER SERIES MAIN CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Rigid headstock for heavy-duty cutting
  • Open, flat-bed design
  • Long bed lengths for long parts
  • Chucker and Universal type available

Our state-of-the-art Machine Shop offers a wide range of services with the lathe, drilling, cutting, grinding and rolling machines.

Our collection of machines allows us to fabricate, modify and assemble a range of tools for most Oil and Gas Industry purposes. To ensure that our clients get the most precise tools at all times, we are unrelenting in our efforts at acquiring more modern fabricating machines.

With our machines and skillful personnel, we can bore flanges, face flanges, and BOP. We can open small diameter holes, bore mud pumps and make grooves with the Shaper Machine. We make keys and keyholes, convert big flat surfaces. We cut threads and re-thread tubing, drill pipes and casings.

Our Lathe Machines are designed to fabricate high precision tools. With the Lathe Machine, we fabricate stabilizers/sleeves and reduce OD's of stabilizers. We also turn down multiple diameter tools in stages, make shafts, threads, and turn big OD's and OD's.
