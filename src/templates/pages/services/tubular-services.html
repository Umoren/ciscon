Tubular Services

Specialized tubular running and handling solutions for all oilfield applications

Overview

Tubular Services

Ciscon specializes in the running and handling of Oilfield Tubular. Our range of tubular services includes Casing and Tubing make up and break-out, with sizes from 1" to 36" OD.

With our experienced crew and state-of-the-art equipment, we provide efficient and safe tubular handling operations for all your well construction needs.

Our Capabilities

Specialized Equipment

Power Units

Ciscon's Casing and Tubing crew deploy either a 4 or 6 Cylinder Duetz Power Unit or an Electrical Power Unit which can deliver up to 3,000psi to our different hydraulic power tongs. CISCON has now standardized on the CLINCH Hydraulic Power Tongs from above 2" to 30" for better performance.

Handling Equipment

With our Flush Mounter Spider and other heavy equipment handling tools, we are capable of handling strings of up to 750 tons and bail up to 32 feet in depth. We deploy our FLUSH MOUNTED SPIDER (FMS) regularly for easier casing handling on all our rig engagements.

Fill-up and Circulation

A Quick Stab Top Drive Fill-up and Circulating Tools (FAC Tool) from 7" to 20" OD casing sizes, are available for filling casing and establishing circulation during casing operations.

Completion Operations

Tubing and Completion Solutions

Ciscon's Tubular unit handles all sizes of tubing during the Completion of any well with an array of equipment such as Dual Tool, thread protectors, spiders/elevators, drifts, stabbing guides, lifting nubbing, slips, pickup elevators, hydraulic and manual tongs and torque turn computers.

Computerized Torque Turn

Our computerized Torque Turn equipment is used to run tubulars with premium threads. In this case, the computer monitors and records the torque and turn values and the maximum make-up RPM which automatically signals the status of the connection against a known "finger print" for the particular thread and size of the tubing.

Intelligent Completion

In the Intelligent Completion Mode, Ciscon provides the patented Multiple Control Line Running System (MCLRS) in technical alliance with TESCO for running tubing with multiple control lines. This system allows for the running of up to 6 control lines and flat packs on the tubing without the possibility of damaging the control lines.
