Intelligent Well Completion

Ciscon offers the latest in intelligent Well Completion technology based on the NESCOS Smart Well Solution. The NESCOS solution (Non-Electrical Surface Controlled Operation System) is an integrated advance well completion system for flow control in hydrocarbon producing and water injection wells.

This system features the new and reliable Rotating sleeve and Choking Devices, hydraulically or mechanically activated to open, close or choke down flow from up to six production zones in a well.

This unique technology is available for you at Ciscon in technical alliance with Precision Completion and Triangle Equipment AS Norway.
